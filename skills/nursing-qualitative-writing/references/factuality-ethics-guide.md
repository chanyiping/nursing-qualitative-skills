# Factuality and ethics guide

## Do not fabricate

Do not invent:

- participant numbers;
- demographic characteristics;
- recruitment sites;
- interview dates or duration;
- inclusion or exclusion criteria;
- sampling strategy;
- interview guide content;
- researcher characteristics;
- reflexive practices;
- analytic software;
- coding process;
- themes or subthemes;
- participant quotations;
- ethics approval numbers;
- informed consent procedures;
- saturation or information power;
- member checking;
- peer debriefing;
- references;
- journal requirements.

## If information is missing

Flag it clearly and use placeholders such as:

- [sample size]
- [setting]
- [interview period]
- [analytic method]
- [ethics approval number]
- [participant quotation]
